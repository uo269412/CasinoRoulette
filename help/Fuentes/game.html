Playing the game

In order to play the game you must place as many bets as you want or can, and later initiate the roulette. When the winners are known, you must redeem your chips, thus ending the round


The window in which the game is shown, offers three tabs the user may switch when they are enabled, they being:

  • Make a bets tab, used to place the bets
  • Your bets tab, that display the information about the bets you have placed
  • Game results tab, which stay hidden until the user decides to play, which shows the results of the game
