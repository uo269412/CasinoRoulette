Order drinks from the application

To order a number of drinks, first you'll need to start choosing and adding to the order the drinks you want to buy

You can filter the drinks by alcoholic or not. Furthermore, you are able to choose if you want ice with your drink or if you want it warm.



When you finally chose the drink. Click on the button add drink to order. The list in the left will be updated and the price of the order will appear

Take into account that the price will appear in green if you have enough money, if you don't, it will be red


If you want to remove a drink from the order, just select it in the list of the left and click on the remove from order button

If you want to cancel the order, just click in the cancel order button


When you finally added to the order all the drinks you want to buy, click on the confirm order button and the order will be processed!
